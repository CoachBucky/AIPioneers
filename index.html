The Pioneers of Artificial Intelligence

An AI Pioneer is a visionary thinker, scientist, or engineer whose foundational ideas and groundbreaking work carved the path for modern artificial intelligence. They challenged the limits of computation, explored the nature of thought, and built the conceptual and practical tools that allow machines to learn, reason, and perceive. This collection celebrates their enduring legacy.
